## Step 4: Get set up for the API {#api}

Go to **Dashboard** > **Developers** > **API** to get your [OAuth 2.0 credentials](/dev-tools/using-api/authentication) and [add your redirect URIs](/dev-tools/using-api/authentication#tokens-user-uri).

![screenshot of Dashboard API page](../../images/topics/welcome/get-started-uri.png)

## Step 5: Add your first account {#account}

1. Create an onboarding link to onboard an [individual](/topics/onboarding/individual/guide-create) or [company](/topics/onboarding/company/guide-create) account holder.
1. Log into Swan's provided Web Banking app. Get the link from your **Dashboard** > **Settings** > **Web Banking**, in the section titled **URLs**.

## Step 6: Validate your account {#validate}

1. Complete [identification](/topics/users/identifications/).
1. Verify the [account holder](/topics/onboarding/account-holders/#verification-process).
1. Submit [supporting documents](/topics/accounts/documents/) if required.

## Step 7: Execute your first payment {#payment}

1. Credit your account. In the Sandbox, you'll do this with fake money through the [Event Simulator](/dev-tools/tools/event-simulator).
    - [Follow the example](/dev-tools/tools/event-simulator#example) on the simulator page to receive an incoming transfer to add fake money to your new Swan account.
1. Use your new fake funds to [initiate a SEPA Credit Transfer](/topics/payments/credit-transfers/sepa/guide-initiate-ct).
1. Use the Event Simulator to book the transfer. Go to **Dashboard** > **Developers** > **Event Simulator**, choose **book an outgoing transfer**, and enter the required information.

:::tip Swan is happy to have you here.

- The world of Swan is now your oyster.
    - Explore the documentation to see all Swan offers.
    - Discover more in the Event Simulator and the [API Explorer](/dev-tools/tools/api-explorer).
- If you're not in communication with Swan (yet), feel free to [submit this form](https://www.swan.io/talk-to-expert) for more information.
- If you already have dedicated Account Managers, please contact them with any questions.

**Happy building!**
:::
