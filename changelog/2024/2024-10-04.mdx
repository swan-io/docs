### New features

🎨 We updated your Dashboard interface for [capital deposit documents](/topics/capital-deposits/#documents) to improve readability and provide easier access to information.

💳 The [transaction reason code enum `InvalidSecurityNumber`](https://api-reference.swan.io/enums/transaction-reason-code/) is back by popular demand.
We previously deprecated it, but we reversed our decision following your feedback.

### Upcoming breaking changes

⛔ In **2 months**, we're removing the [`user.idVerified` field](https://api-reference.swan.io/objects/user#useridverifiedboolean---).
Please start using the [new field `user.identificationsLevels.expert`](https://api-reference.swan.io/objects/user#useridverifiedboolean---).

ℹ️ In **4 weeks**, the [`cardDetails.Card` field](https://api-reference.swan.io/unions/card-details/) will no longer be mandatory.
