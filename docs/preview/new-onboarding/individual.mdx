### Tax information

| Field | FR | DE | IT | NL | ES | BE | Frontend | Sign-up |
|---|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| `taxIdentificationNumber` | Cond.¹ | ✓ | ✓ | Cond.¹ | Cond.¹ | ✓ | Cond.¹ | Cond.¹ |

¹ `taxIdentificationNumber` is required if any of the following conditions are true:

- `accountAdmin.address.country` is different from `accountInfo.country`.
- `accountAdmin.address.country` is Germany, Italy, or Belgium.
- `accountInfo.country` is undefined.
