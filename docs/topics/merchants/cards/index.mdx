# Cards for merchants

:::caution beta
Accepting payments with cards is in private beta.
:::

Merchants can accept card payments with a payment link.

## About card payments for merchants {#about}

Cards are the most common way for customers worldwide to pay.
Swan supports all major card issuers, including Visa, Mastercard, AmericanExpress, and *Carte Bleue* (CB) in France.

Merchants can use card payment links to accept payments from card holders anywhere.
Collect rent, share invoices, and even get paid with a Swan merchant account.

## Settlement date and booked time {#settlement}

Merchant card transactions are booked the **business day after the transaction is captured** at **20:00** Central European [Summer] Time (CET/CEST) (18:00 Coordinated Universal Time [UTC]).

Therefore, assuming both Tuesday and Wednesday are business days, transactions captured on Tuesday between 00:00 (midnight) and 23:59 UTC are booked on Wednesday.

### Chargebacks & disputes {#disputes}

Card holders might create chargebacks, which opens a dispute.
Merchants can view the dispute automatically; both the transaction status and the merchant's balance change.

To respond to disputes, ask your merchant to email Swan Support at support@swan.io.
You can also open a support ticket from your Dashboard.
