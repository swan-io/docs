# Preview

Swan's preview gives you visibility into our development roadmap, helping you plan integrations and stay ahead of platform changes.

## Category types

**Preview** - Early implementations that will require integration work. Use these to prepare your codebase and provide feedback before general availability.

**Beta** - Features in limited testing. Functionality may change based on feedback.

**Upcoming** - Detailed specifications for features in development. No implementation required - use these for planning and architectural decisions.
