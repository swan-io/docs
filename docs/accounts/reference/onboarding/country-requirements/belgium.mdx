# 🇧🇪 Belgium

## Companies {#companies}

### Legal forms {#company-types}

| Legal form (Dutch/French) | Description |
|---|---|
| Besloten Vennootschap (BV) / Société à Responsabilité Limitée (SRL) | Private Limited Liability Company |
| Naamloze Vennootschap (NV) / Société Anonyme (SA) | Public Limited Company |
| Vennootschap onder Firma (VOF) / Société en Nom Collectif (SNC) | General Partnership |
| Commanditaire Vennootschap (CommV) / Société en Commandite Simple (SCS) | Limited Partnership |
| Coöperatieve Vennootschap (CV) / Société Coopérative (SC) | Cooperative Company |
| Maatschap / Société Simple | Private Partnership without legal personality |

### Accepted documents {#company-documents}

| Document type (Dutch/French) | Description | Details |
|---|---|---|
| Uittreksel Handelsregister / Extrait Banque-Carrefour des Entreprises (BCE)* | Proof of Registration* | <ul><li>Must be official and dated.</li><li>Retrieved by Swan from the Crossroads Bank for Enterprises (CBE)</li><li>Must be dated within the last 3 months.</li></ul> |
| Akte van Oprichting / Statuts | Articles of Association | <ul><li>Must be the most recently published version, or dated within the last **6 months** if amendments have been made.</li><li>Retrieved from CBE → Publications in National Gazette **or** Moniteur Belge direct search.</li><li>Dated and signed.</li></ul> |
| Uittreksel UBO-register / Extrait registre UBO | UBO register extract | <ul><li>From UBO Register (requires NBB authorization) **or** company provision.</li><li>Mandatory under Belgian law.</li></ul> |
| Beëdigde verklaring / Déclaration sur l'honneur | Sworn statement | <ul><li>Company declaration.</li><li>If UBO register extract is unavailable.</li><li>Must be signed and dated within the last 3 months.</li></ul> |
| Identiteitsdocument / Pièce d'identité | Identity documents of directors | <ul><li>Identity document or passport of directors.</li><li>All directors who can represent company.</li><li>Must be submitted in color, both sides (if applicable).</li></ul> |
| Identiteitsdocument UBO's / Pièce d'identité des UBOs | UBO identity documents | <ul><li>For all identified UBOs.</li><li>All valid identity documents are accepted.</li><li>Must be in color (both sides if applicable).</li></ul> |
| Bewijs van adres / Justificatif de domicile | Proof of address of UBOs | <ul><li>Must be dated within the last 3 months.</li></ul> |
| Volmacht / Procuration | Power of Attorney (if applicable) | <ul><li>From authorized directors/Board of Directors.</li><li>Must grant power to open *and* manage accounts.</li><li>Must be dated (within the last year) and signed.</li><li>Identity document required if the legal representative is not the account holder.</li></ul> |
| Financiële overzichten / Comptes annuels | Annual accounts or tax returns | <ul><li>Retrieved from NBB Central Balance Sheet Office.</li><li>Must be the most recent.</li><li>For enhanced due diligence.</li></ul> |
| Belastingaangifte / Déclaration fiscale | Company's tax return | <ul><li>To be provided by company.</li><li>Additional financial verification.</li></ul> |
| Organigram / Organigramme | Company structure chart | <ul><li>To be provided by company.</li><li>Document must be authentic and the latest version.</li><li>For complex ownership structures.</li></ul> |

## Self-employed individuals {#self-employed}

### Legal forms {#self-employed-types}

| Legal form (Dutch/French) | Description |
|---|---|
| Eenmanszaak / Entreprise individuelle | Self-employed |

### Accepted documents {#self-employed-documents}

| Document type (Dutch/French) | Description | Details |
|---|---|---|
| Uittreksel Handelsregister / Extrait BCE* | Proof of Registration* | <ul><li>Must be official and dated.</li><li>Retrieved by Swan from the Crossroads Bank for Enterprises (CBE).</li><li>Must show: name, address, registration number, activity description.</li></ul> |
| Identiteitsdocument / Pièce d'identité* | Identity document of individual* | <ul><li>Standard ID verification.</li><li>Individual represents themselves - no separate legal entity.</li><li>Must be submitted in color, both sides (if applicable).</li></ul> |
| Eerste overschrijving / Premier transfert* | First transfer* | <ul><li>For self-employed (entrepreneur individuel) individuals only.</li><li>Must make a first transfer into their payment account from another bank account registered in their name.</li><li>Bank account must be within the European Economic Area (EEA).</li></ul> |

### Accepted documents {#association-documents}

| Document type (Dutch/French) | Description | Details |
|---|---|---|
| Uittreksel Handelsregister / Extrait BCE* | Proof of Registration* | <ul><li>Must be official and dated.</li><li>Retrieved by Swan from the Crossroads Bank for Enterprises (CBE).</li></ul> |
| Akte van Oprichting / Statuts | Articles of Association | <ul><li>Must be dated and signed.</li><li>Must be the most recently published version.</li><li>If amendments have been made, it must be dated within the last **6 months**.</li><li>Must include company name, objectives, and management structure.</li></ul> |
| Notulen vergadering / Procès verbal* | Latest meeting minutes* | <ul><li>Must be the most recent.</li><li>Signed by attendees.</li></ul> |
| Identiteitsdocument / Pièce d'identité | Identity document of UBOs | <ul><li>All valid identity documents are accepted.</li><li>Must be in color (both sides if applicable).</li></ul> |
| KVK uittreksel UBO-register / Extrait registre UBO | Ultimate Beneficial Owner (UBO) certificate | <ul><li>Official document signed and stamped.</li><li>Requested if not clearly shown on proof of registration.</li><li>Screenshots of the UBO declaration are not accepted.</li></ul> |
| Bewijs van adres / Justificatif de domicile | Proof of address of UBOs | <ul><li>Must be dated within the last 3 months.</li></ul> |
| Bewijs van adres / Justificatif de l'adresse de la société | Proof of company address | <ul><li>Must be dated within the last 3 months.</li></ul> |
| Aangifte vennootschapsbelasting / Avis d'imposition | Business income tax return | <ul><li>Most recent (for the previous year).</li></ul> |
| Volmacht / Procuration | Power of Attorney and identity document of the legal representative | <ul><li>Must be dated (within the last year) and signed.</li><li>Identity document required if the legal representative is not the account holder.</li><li>Must be submitted in color, both sides (if applicable).</li></ul> |

## Homeowner associations {#homeowners-associations}

### Legal forms {#homeowner-association-types}

| Legal form (Dutch/French) | Description |
|---|---|
| Vereniging van Mede-eigenaars / Association de copropriétaires (ACP) | Homeowners association (HOA) |

### Accepted documents {#homeowner-association-documents}

| Document type (Dutch/French) | Description | Details |
|---|---|---|
| Uittreksel Handelsregister / Extrait BCE* | Proof of Registration* | <ul><li>From CBE Public Search using address or registration number - save as PDF.</li><li>⚠️ **Note:** Some HOAs not yet registered in CBE.</li></ul> |
| Basisakte en appartementsreglement / Acte de base et règlement de copropriété | Deed of Co-ownership and Internal Regulations | <ul><li>To be provided by the HOA.</li><li>Must be the most recently published version, or dated within the last **6 months** if amendments have been made.</li><li>Fundamental governing documents.</li></ul> |
| Notulen vergadering / Procès verbal* | Latest meeting minutes* | <ul><li>To be provided by HOA.</li><li>Shows current decisions and authority.</li><li>Must be the most recent.</li><li>Must be signed by assembly president, secretary, and co-owners present.</li></ul> |
| Identiteitsdocument / Pièce d'identité* | Identity document of Syndic* | <ul><li>All valid identity documents are accepted.</li><li>Syndic is sole legal representative.</li><li>Must be a valid identity document.</li><li>Must be submitted in color, both sides (if applicable).</li></ul> |
| Professionele referenties / Attestation professionnelle | Professional credentials | <ul><li>IPI number verification from IPI website.</li><li>IPI number format: 6 digits (e.g., 505.833).</li><li>Verification if professional Syndic.</li></ul> |
| Financiële verklaring / Compte financier | Financial statement | <ul><li>To be provided by HOA.</li><li>For enhanced due diligence.</li></ul> |
| Volmacht / Procuration | Power of attorney from Syndic | <ul><li>From Syndic only.</li><li>⚠️ Syndic cannot delegate without general assembly agreement.</li><li>Proof of general assembly authorization for delegation required.</li><li>Must be dated (within the last year) and signed.</li></ul> |

| Document type (Dutch/French) | Description | Details |
|---|---|---|
| Uittreksel Handelsregister / Extrait BCE* | Proof of Registration* | <ul><li>Must be official and dated.</li><li>Retrieved by Swan from the Crossroads Bank for Enterprises (CBE).</li></ul> |

| Notulen vergadering / Procès-verbal | Meeting minutes, house rules, member list, voting records, or board member documents | <ul><li>Only required once members or investors are onboarded.</li><li>Must be the latest versions.</li><li>Signed by attendees.</li><li>Not mandatory for cooperatives less than 1 year old.</li></ul> |
| Bewijs van adres / Justificatif de domicile | Proof of address | <ul><li>Must be dated within the last 3 months.</li></ul> |
| Financiële overzichten / États financiers | Financial statements | <ul><li>Most recent annual report or balance sheet.</li><li>Dated within the last 3 months.</li></ul> |
| Volmacht / Procuration | Power of Attorney and identity document of the legal representative | <ul><li>Must be dated (within the last year) and signed.</li><li>Identity document required if the legal representative is not the account holder.</li></ul> |
| Identiteitsdocument / Pièce d'identité | Identity document of UBOs | <ul><li>All valid identity documents are accepted.</li><li>Must be in color (both sides if applicable).</li></ul> |
| Bewijs van adres / Justificatif de domicile | Proof of address of UBOs | <ul><li>Must be dated within the last 3 months.</li></ul> |
