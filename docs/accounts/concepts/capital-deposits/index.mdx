# Capital deposits

<p className="ia-lede">A <Term id="capital-deposit">capital deposit</Term> collects a new company's share capital and produces the certificate it needs to register, the upstream step before its account holder is onboarded. Currently available in 🇫🇷 France.</p>

It's the first step of [company onboarding](/accounts/guides/onboarding/company): you deposit the capital and obtain the certificate before the company is legally registered, then onboard it as an account holder.
