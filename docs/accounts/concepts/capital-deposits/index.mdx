# Capital deposits

<p className="ia-lede">A capital deposit collects a new company's share capital and produces the certificate it needs to register — the upstream step before its account holder is onboarded. Currently available in 🇫🇷 France.</p>

> <CapitalDepositDefinition />

It's the first step of [company onboarding](/accounts/guides/onboarding/company): you deposit the capital and obtain the certificate before the company is legally registered, then onboard it as an account holder.

## How capital deposit fits

A capital deposit produces the certificate a new company needs before it can be onboarded as an account holder and receive its Swan account.
