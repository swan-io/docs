## New features

📥 You can now [export card data](/topics/cards/overview/guide-export/) in `.csv` format from your Dashboard or with the API by calling the `exportCardData` mutation.

🔔 You can now activate two [new text message notifications](/get-started/set-up-swan/create-project#notifications-text-message) for rejected card payments due to an **incorrect PIN** or an **invalid card expiration date**. 
Activate them from your **Dashboard** > **Settings** > **Notifications**.

💌 A new white-labeled [email notification](/get-started/set-up-swan/create-project#notifications-email) for card expiration is now available! 
Activate it from your **Dashboard** > **Settings** > **Notifications**.

## Update

🔴 To provide more detailed feedback, errors in the `addCards` inputs, especially the [physical card delivery address](https://api-reference.swan.io/inputs/print-physical-card-input/) and [spending limit](https://api-reference.swan.io/objects/spending-limit/), will now return a `ValidationRejection` instead of an error.
