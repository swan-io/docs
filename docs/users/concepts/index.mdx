# Users concepts

<p className="ia-lede">The models behind Swan users: the person identified by their phone number, the consent that authorizes sensitive operations, and the identifications that prove who they are.</p>

## What makes up a Swan user

A Swan user is the person at the centre. Identification proves who they are, and consent authorizes the sensitive operations they perform.
