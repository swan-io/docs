## New features

🔔 [Physical cards](/topics/cards/physical/guide-activate#activation-reminder-notifications) that haven't been activated after their estimated delivery date now trigger automatic reminder emails to cardholders. Reminders are sent at configurable intervals until the card is activated. Configure this from your **Dashboard > Settings > Notifications**.

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Account administrator changes are now easier to handle for your customers. A dedicated request form automatically collects the right supporting documents based on company type, reducing back-and-forth and speeding up processing. Share the guided form directly with your customers.

## Improvements

📫 Swan now requires email address verification when creating a new project in Sandbox. This limits impersonation risk and reinforces security across all projects.

🛡️ The Web Banking sign-up flow has been updated to strengthen protections against social engineering and fraud. Users now complete sign-up on desktop, entering a 6-digit SMS verification code and their personal information there, then switch to mobile to complete identification via a link sent to their device. ### Upcoming breaking changes
