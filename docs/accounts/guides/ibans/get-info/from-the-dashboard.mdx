# Get IBAN information from the Dashboard

View an account's main and virtual IBANs from your Dashboard.

## Steps

1. Go to **Dashboard** > **Data** > **Accounts**.
1. Open the account whose IBANs you want to review.
1. Open the **Virtual IBANs** tab to see the account's virtual IBANs.
