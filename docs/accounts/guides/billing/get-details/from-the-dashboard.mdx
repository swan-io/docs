# Review invoice details on the Dashboard

Review the details of a single invoice on your Dashboard.

## Steps

1. Go to **Dashboard** > **Data** > **Accounts**.
1. Open the account for which you want to review invoices.
1. Go to the **Invoices** tab and find the invoice you'd like to review.
