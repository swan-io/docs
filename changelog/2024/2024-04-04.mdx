### New features

🌎  Account holders can now send [International Credit Transfers](/payments/concepts/credit-transfers/international) to:

- Exchange euros for another currency and transfer it.
- Transfer euros outside of SEPA.
- Receive International Credit Transfers in euros sent from outside of SEPA.

Please contact your Account Manager if you're interested.

### Improvements

✅ [Beneficiary verification](/payments/concepts/credit-transfers/sepa/#beneficiary-verification) between two Dutch accounts is now available on Swan's Web Banking.
