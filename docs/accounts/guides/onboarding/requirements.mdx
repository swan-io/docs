# Requirements

## Country requirements {#country-reqs}

Onboarding is a **highly localized experience**.
Everything from **what to include in your API request** to how to ask **users to verify their identity** to whether Swan **requires certain ID numbers or documents** depends on the [account country](/accounts/concepts/account/country).

Account onboarding country requirements are described in the [individual](/accounts/guides/onboarding/individual/requirements#country-reqs) and [company](/accounts/guides/onboarding/company/requirements#country-reqs) onboarding sections.
Please follow the requirements for your target countries closely.
