## New features

🧭 The new [Onboarding API](/preview/new-onboarding/) is now live in beta. It improves data collection for better risk assessment and regulatory compliance, and delivers a cleaner developer experience with structured inputs, typed responses, and updated mutations and queries. Documentation is fully updated to reflect all new validations.

## Improvements

🔎 [`CapitalDepositDocumentRefusedStatusInfo`](/topics/capital-deposits/) now includes an optional `reason` string field with a free-text explanation of why a document was refused. You can view the exact explanation in your dashboard, alongside the existing `reasonCode` value.

🔍 [Account holders](/topics/onboarding/account-holders/) can now be [filtered](https://api-reference.swan.io/inputs/account-holder-filter-input/) by their KYC [verification requirements](https://api-reference.swan.io/enums/verification-requirement-type/), both in the Dashboard and via the API. Supported filters include `FirstTransferRequired`, `SupportingDocumentsRequired`, `UboDetailsRequired`, `LegalRepresentativeDetailsRequired`, `OrganizationDetailsRequired`, `TaxIdRequired`, and `Other`.

## API updates

### Reminders

⚠️ Starting **April 2**, four [`CloseAccountReasonType`](https://api-reference.swan.io/enums/close-account-reason-type/) values are replaced with more granular codes. The deprecated values are `ComplianceReason`, `PartnerReason`, `Inactivity`, and `CapitalDepositReason`, previously used for account opening refusals, capital deposit cancellations, and retroactive account closures.

Accounts closed before **April 2** keep their original reason codes. Refer to the [deprecated reason codes](/changelog/26-february-2026/#upcoming-breaking-changes) for the full mapping.

👉 Check all reminders of already-announced upcoming breaking changes on [docs.swan.io/changelog](/changelog).

## Coming up

🏛️ In April, we're launching a new Partner-branded Re-KYC portal. It enables account administrators to complete periodic KYC reviews through a multilingual interface with pre-filled data and automated email reminders.

💻 A new desktop sign-up flow for Web Banking is coming soon. Authentication and personal information will be completed on desktop: users receive a 6-digit OTP code by SMS, enter the code, create a passcode, then enter personal information before switching to mobile to complete identification. This keeps authentication on a single device, reducing exposure to phishing and social engineering attacks. No changes are required on your side.

👀 Starting **April 2**, [`CapitalDepositCaseCanceledStatusInfo`](/topics/capital-deposits/) gains a cancelation reason code when the case status is `Canceled`. You can also subscribe to the `CapitalDepositCaseEvent.Canceled` webhook to receive notifications when a capital deposit case is canceled.

Capital deposit cases canceled before **April 2** have a `null` cancelation reason at the capital deposit level; for those cases, refer to the account-level deprecated closure reason `CapitalDepositReason`.
