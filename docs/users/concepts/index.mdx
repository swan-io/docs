# Users concepts

<p className="ia-lede">Understanding Swan users.</p>

## What makes up a Swan user

The user is the person at the centre of your integration.

Their phone number identifies them; identification proves who they are.<br/>
Explicit consent authorizes the sensitive operations they perform.<br/>
