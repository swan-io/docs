# Insurance and claims

<p className="ia-lede">Coverage included with each card package tier, and how users report and file insurance claims.</p>

Insurance coverage is determined by the selected [card package](/cards/concepts/card-packages#insurance) tier. When a card is issued with a package that includes insurance, Swan activates coverage with our insurance partner if the prerequisites are met.

## Insurance claims {#insurance-claims}

Depending on the type of claim, your user can seek support through Swan, our insurance claims portal, or our emergency assistance provider.

### Reporting fraud to Swan {#report-to-swan}

Fraud and phishing, vishing, and smishing are reported directly to Swan within two days.
When a user reports fraud or phishing, vishing, and smishing, we secure the account or card and guide them through the next steps.

:::info Card fraud dispute end user support article
Share the dedicated [Support Center article](https://support.swan.io/hc/en-150/articles/17099978406045-Credit-transfer-and-card-payment-fraud-disputes#h_01HQHA9YY8M234ZE7AV1GY6APF) to help your users file their fraud dispute.
:::

### Filing a claim through the portal {#claims-portal}

In most cases, users should submit the claim through the insurance claims portal in their banking app.

To submit a claim, users must:
1. Access the insurance claims portal using their **Swan account email address**. Swan automatically creates an account for the user containing all their covered cards.
1. **Set a password** when setting up their account for the first time.
1. **Upload the required documents** directly to the portal.

Our **insurance provider manages the entire claims process** after submission.
Users must follow up and track the progress of their claim directly through the portal.
If our insurance provider approves the claim, they credit it directly to the cardholder's Swan account.

:::info Customized claims portal
By default, our insurance provider co-brands the claims management interface with your **logo and accent color**, like your Swan interfaces (Web Banking and consent screens).
:::

### Calling emergency assistance (Premium only)

Users with a **Premium** card can get urgent help by calling our assistance provider.
The phone number is listed in their banking app.

Our assistance provider manages the entire claims process.
If they approve the claim, they credit it directly to the cardholder's Swan account.

The following table outlines how to report different claim types.

    |---|---|---|
    | Bank card fraud | Report to Swan within two business days. | - Police report<br />- [Fraudulent usage declaration form](https://support.swan.io/hc/en-150/articles/17099978406045-Credit-transfer-and-card-payment-fraud-disputes#h_01HQHA9YY8M234ZE7AV1GY6APF)<br />- Transaction details<br />- Screenshot of disputed transactions |
