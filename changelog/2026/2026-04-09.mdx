## New features

🔔 [Physical cards](/topics/cards/physical/guide-activate#activation-reminder-notifications) that haven't been activated after their estimated delivery date now trigger automatic reminder emails to cardholders. Reminders are sent at configurable intervals until the card is activated. Configure this from your **Dashboard > Settings > Notifications**.

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 [Changing account administrator](/topics/accounts/memberships/admin-change) is now easier to handle for your customers. The dedicated request form determines which supporting documents are required based on company type and reason for change, so requesters upload the right documents on the first attempt. Share the guided form directly with your customers.

## Improvements

📫 Swan now requires email address verification when creating a new project in Sandbox. This limits impersonation risk and reinforces security across all projects.

🛡️ The Web Banking sign-up flow has been updated to strengthen protections against social engineering and fraud. Users now complete sign-up on desktop by entering a 6-digit SMS verification code and their personal information. They then switch to mobile to complete identification via a link sent to their device. ### Upcoming breaking changes

📆 On **April 30**, the deprecated `InternalCreditTransfer` value will be removed from the [`PaymentProduct`](https://api-reference.swan.io/enums/payment-product/) enum. Remove any references to this value from your transaction queries and filtering before this date.
