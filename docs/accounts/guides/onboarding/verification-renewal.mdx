# Verification renewal (Re-KYC)

As required by financial regulations, Swan periodically verifies <Term id="account-holder">account holder</Term> information.
Verification renewal applies to all accounts held by the account holder.

It is a mandatory process that happens every 1, 3, or 5 years, or when we detect changes in an account holder's profile.

:::warning Account suspension
Users must confirm their account holder information within **90 days**. If they don't, we automatically suspend their accounts and restrict all related IBANs, cards, and payment capabilities until they complete verification.
:::

## Completing verification renewal in the portal {#verification-renewal-portal}

Swan provides a no-code, partner-branded portal to handle the verification renewal process. You don't need to integrate with an API. 

Make sure your [branding settings](/get-started/set-up-swan/create-project#step-8-configure-your-branding-in-live) (logo and accent color) are up to date in your **Dashboard** > **Settings** > **Branding**.

### Verification renewal steps {#verification-renewal-portal-steps}

The <Term id="legal-representative">legal representative</Term> (account admin):

1. Receives the [initial notification email](#verification-renewal-timeline) with a unique portal link.
1. Opens the link and provides consent to access the portal.
1. Reviews the pre-filled information.
1. Confirms the information is correct, or updates it if anything has changed.
1. Uploads [supporting documents](/accounts/concepts/documents#required-documents) if required.
1. Submits the form and receives a confirmation email.

The KYC analyst reviews the submission.
If more information is needed, the KYC analyst sends a follow-up request with a new portal link.
The portal reopens for the missing items only.

After Swan validates the review, the account holder's [`VerificationRenewalStatus`](https://api-reference.swan.io/enums/verification-renewal-status/) changes to `Completed`. Verification renewal doesn't impact the account holder's banking verification status.

## Verification renewal timeline and communication {#verification-renewal-timeline}

:::tip Update your communication preferences
We send the initial form and all follow-ups based on your [communication preferences](/accounts/guides/onboarding/account-holders#notifications). 
We recommend that Swan emails your end users directly and copies you (CC). You can also specify a unique email address. 
Create a [support ticket](https://partner-support.swan.io/hc/fr/requests/new) that outlines your communication preferences for verification renewal.
:::

We start the **90-day** period when we send the account holder the first notification.
At the 90-day deadline, Swan suspends all of the account holder's accounts if they haven't completed verification renewal.
To lift the suspension, the user can complete the process using the last portal link Swan emailed them. 
Afterwards, either you or the user must submit a support request for a review. 
The KYC analyst will review the suspension reason and the updated information.

Share [Swan's Support Center article](https://support.swan.io/hc/en-150/articles/26326687003549-Confirm-personal-details-again) to show them the full timeline and the reminders we send.
