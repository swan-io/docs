# Multiple accounts

<p className="ia-lede">Create additional Swan accounts for an existing company <Term id="account-holder">account holder</Term> without repeating onboarding or Know Your Business (KYB) verification.</p>

:::info Specific coverage 
Contact your Strategic Account Manager (SAM) to request the Multiple Accounts feature.
:::

## Overview {#overview}

**The Multiple Accounts feature allows a single company to create additional Swan accounts** under the same legal entity. These accounts share the same registration number and [local IBAN](/accounts/concepts/ibans/local) as the first account created during onboarding.

Each account operates independently with its own <Term id="ibans">IBAN</Term>, balances, and transaction flows, making it easy to create additional accounts for specific business needs. 

They have the same capabilities as regular accounts, including issuing and accepting all payment types (e.g., [card payments](/payments/concepts/cards/), [SEPA Credit Transfers](/payments/concepts/credit-transfers/sepa/)). 

To accept payments, each account requires its own [merchant profile review](/payments/concepts/merchants#profiles), so a new profile and payment method must be requested for each additional account created.

## Eligibility {#eligibility}

The Multiple Accounts feature is available for **[company account](/accounts/guides/onboarding/company) types**, which include:

- Registered companies
- Self-employed workers
- Associations or non-profit organizations

:::info Individual accounts
Individuals may create additional accounts. A new onboarding process is required, but Know Your Customer (KYC) isn't repeated.
:::

## Membership management

Memberships are tied to each account, not to the account holder. 
Even if a user is a member of the first account created during onboarding, they must be invited to every new account. 
For example, an employee can access the operating account (created first), but not the new reserve account.

[Learn more about account memberships →](/accounts/concepts/memberships)
