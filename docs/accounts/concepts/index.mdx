# Accounts concepts

<p className="ia-lede">The models behind Swan accounts: from onboarding and account holders through the account itself, its memberships, IBANs, funding, billing, supporting documents, and closure.</p>

## The account model

Onboarding creates a verified account holder, who owns the Swan account. Everything else hangs off that account: who can use it, how money moves, how it scales, and how it closes.
