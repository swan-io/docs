<details>
  <summary>⚠️ **Upcoming breaking changes**</summary>

  <div>

    📆 On **May 20, 2026**, the [consent notification endpoint](/topics/users/consent/guide-configure-notifications) timeout will be reduced from 3 seconds to 2 seconds. If your integration uses in-app consent notifications, make sure your endpoint responds within 2 seconds. Otherwise, Swan falls back to text message. OAuth2 flows are not affected.

    📆 On **May 21, 2026**, public onboarding URLs will switch to the [current onboarding API](/accounts/guides/onboarding) for all new onboardings, and the previous public onboarding interface will be retired. Action is only required if you prefill onboarding fields through the API before redirecting to the onboarding URL.

    📆 On **June 4, 2026**, deprecated `Card`-named types and fields used for [accepting online card payments](/topics/merchants/online/cards/) will be removed. Update your queries and mutations to use the new `OnlineCard`-named equivalents before this date. Card issuing is unchanged.

    📆 On **September 30, 2026**, the [previous onboarding API](/preview/new-onboarding/legacy-onboarding/) will be shut down and all previous endpoints will no longer be available. Follow the [migration guide](/accounts/guides/onboarding/migrate-api) to move to the [current onboarding API](/accounts/guides/onboarding) before this date.
