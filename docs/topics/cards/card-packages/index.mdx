### Overview

Card packages are ready-made, tailored offers that help you launch market-ready card products faster. 

When you create a card product, you select a package. 
Packages come in three tiers: Standard, Essential, and Premium, and include pre-built features and settings.

### Card package comparison

| Feature | Standard tier | Essential tier | Premium tier |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Insurance coverage | Standard | Essential | Premium |
| Spending limits | €10,000 | €40,000 | €100,000 |
| Foreign exchange (FX) fees | 2% | 1% | 0% |
| Card design* | Plastic (black or silver) | Plastic (black or silver) | Metal |

*All tiers support branded and [custom](/topics/cards/design/#custom) card designs.

:::info Existing cards
You can't add card packages to existing cards.
If you want to offer a card package to existing users, create a new card product and issue new cards.
:::

### Configure a card product {#card-product-configuration}

When you create a card product, you select a package. 
Once set, the package automatically links to newly issued cards. 
Read our step-by-step guide to learn how to [configure your card product](/topics/cards/card-packages/guide-product-configuration).

## Card insurance {#insurance}

The insurance coverage offered depends on the card package you select for your card product. 
To provide coverage, issue a new card with a package.

### Insurance subscription {#insurance-subscription}

:::tip Prerequisites
The creation of a subscription may be **delayed** until these conditions are met:
- Account membership: `Enabled`
- Account holder verification status: `Verified` 
:::

Swan handles the insurance subscription with our insurance partner. 
No action is required from you. 

The subscription progresses through the following statuses: `Pending` → `Processing` → `Enabled`, or `Canceled`.

The card status determines the insurance status. It's `Enabled` when a card is ordered and `Canceled` when the card is canceled.

:::info Card packages activation
Once `Enabled`, the insurance activates automatically. Your user can view the insurance notice and access the insurance claims portal URL in their banking app.
:::

### Card package billing {#package-billing}

Billing for card packages, whether to the partner or your user, depends on your Swan contract, the selected card package, and your chosen [billing model](/topics/accounts/billing/guide-compliant-billing). Fees include:

- **Card subscription**: Monthly fee per active card.
- **FX fees**: Charged per transaction in non-euro currencies.
- **Physical cards**: One-time printing and shipping fee (billed to Partner only).

For more information, see the [usage-based billing metrics](/topics/accounts/billing/#billing-metrics).
For detailed pricing, refer to your T&Cs or [submit a request](https://partner-support.swan.io/hc/fr/requests/new) to Swan Support.

### Card insurance claims {#insurance-claims}

Depending on the type of claim, cardholders can seek support through the Swan, our insurance claims portal, or our emergency assistance provider.

#### Reporting to Swan {#report-to-swan}

Fraud and phishing are reported directly to Swan. 
When a cardholder reports fraud or phishing, we secure the account or card and guide them through the next steps.

:::info Card fraud dispute end user support article
Share the dedicated [Support Center article](https://support.swan.io/hc/en-150/articles/17099978406045-Credit-transfer-and-card-payment-fraud-disputes#h_01HQHA9YY8M234ZE7AV1GY6APF) to help your users file their fraud dispute.
:::

#### Reporting through the claims portal {#claims-portal}

In most cases, cardholders should submit the claim through the insurance claims portal in their banking app. 

To submit a claim, cardholders must:
1. Access the insurance claims portal using their **Swan account email address**. Swan automatically creates an account for the cardholder containing all their covered cards.
1. **Set a password** when setting up their account for the first time.
1. **Upload the required documents** directly to the portal.

Our **insurance partner manages the entire claims process** after submission. 
Cardholders must follow up and track the progress of their claim directly through the portal. 
If our insurance partner approves the claim, they credit it directly to the cardholder's Swan account.

:::info Customized claims portal    
By default, our insurance partner co-brands the claims management interface with your **logo and accent color**, like your Swan interfaces (Web Banking and consent screens).
:::

#### Reporting to our assistance provider

Your users with a **Premium** card can get urgent help by calling our assistance provider. 
The phone number is in their banking app.

Our assistance provider manages the entire claims process. 
If they approve the claim, they credit it directly to the cardholder's Swan account.
