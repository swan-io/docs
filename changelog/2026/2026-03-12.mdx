## Improvements

ūüĒé [`CapitalDepositDocumentRefusedStatusInfo`](/topics/capital-deposits/) now includes an optional `reason` string field with a free-text explanation of why a document was refused. You can view the exact explanation on your Dashboard, alongside the existing `reasonCode` value.

ūüĒć [Account holders](/topics/onboarding/account-holders/) can now be [filtered](https://api-reference.swan.io/inputs/account-holder-filter-input/) by their Know Your Customer (KYC) [verification requirements](https://api-reference.swan.io/enums/verification-requirement-type/), both in the Dashboard and through the API. Supported filters include `FirstTransferRequired`, `SupportingDocumentsRequired`, `UboDetailsRequired`, `LegalRepresentativeDetailsRequired`, `OrganizationDetailsRequired`, `TaxIdRequired`, and `Other`.

ūüĎÄ Starting **April 2**, [`CapitalDepositCaseCanceledStatusInfo`](/topics/capital-deposits/) gains a cancellation reason code when the case status is `Canceled`. You can also subscribe to the `CapitalDepositCaseEvent.Canceled` webhook to receive notifications when a capital deposit case is canceled.

Capital deposit cases canceled before **April 2** have a `null` cancellation reason at the capital deposit level; for those cases, refer to the account-level deprecated closure reason `CapitalDepositReason`.
