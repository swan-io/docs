# Physical cards

<p className="ia-lede">An optional plastic card ordered for a virtual card, usable for swipe, chip and PIN, contactless, and online payments.</p>

<PhysicalCardsDefinition />

You can order a physical card for each virtual card.
Swan physical cards are typical plastic cards that can be used for swipe, chip and PIN, contactless, and online transactions.
Your [design](/cards/concepts/design) is printed on all physical cards.

## Printing physical cards with the API {#mutations}

You can use one of several mutations to print physical cards with the API.

| Mutation | Use case | Guide |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [`printPhysicalCard`](https://api-reference.swan.io/mutations/print-physical-card/) | Print one physical card for one account member. The [virtual card](/cards/concepts/virtual) must already be added. | [Print a physical card](/cards/guides/physical/print#print-one) |
| [`addCards`](https://api-reference.swan.io/mutations/add-cards/) | <AddCardsUseCases /> | [Print a physical card when adding a virtual card](/cards/guides/physical/print#print-with-virtual) |
| [`addCardsWithGroupDelivery`](https://api-reference.swan.io/mutations/add-cards-with-group-delivery/) | Ship a group of physical cards together to the same address. | [Print multiple physical cards with group delivery](/cards/guides/physical/print#print-group) |

<AddCardsAlert />

## Card number {#number}

Each physical card has a unique card number, different even from the virtual card added before the physical card was printed.

Swan makes the physical card's number, CVC, expiry date, and the PIN available in Web Banking.
The **card number**, **CVC**, and **PIN** are considered sensitive data and must be **masked by default**.
The cardholder must complete a **consent request** before the numbers can be unmasked.
