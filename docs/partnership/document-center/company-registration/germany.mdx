# Proof of registration for German companies

:::tip Document retrieval
For companies registered in 🇩🇪 Germany, Swan independently retrieves all required documents. If retrieval isn't possible, Swan may request the documents. For self-employed individuals and freelancers, users must provide all listed mandatory documents themselves.
:::

:::info Mandatory documents
Documents marked with an asterisk (*) are mandatory, meaning they are **required** to verify an account holder, regardless of how they're retrieved. Other documents are optional but may still be retrieved by Swan or requested on a case-by-case basis.
:::

<span>
<p>Documents must be written in the **Latin alphabet**, and must be comprehensive, accurate, and compliant with legal requirements.</p>
</span>

## Companies {#companies}

### Legal forms {#company-types}

| Legal form (German) | Description |
|---|---|
| Offene Handelsgesellschaft (oHG) | General partnership |
| Kommanditgesellschaft (KG) | Limited partnership |
| Gesellschaft mit beschränkter Haftung (GmbH) | Limited Liability Company |
| Gesellschaft mit beschränkter Haftung & Compagnie Kommanditgesellschaft (GmbH & Co. KG) | Limited Partnership with a Limited Liability Company as General Partner |
| Gesellschaft mit beschränkter Haftung & Compagnie offene Handelsgesellschaft (GmbH & Co. oHG) | General Partnership with a Limited Liability Company as General Partner |
| Unternehmergesellschaft (haftungsbeschränkt) (UG) | Entrepreneurial Company (limited liability) |
| Aktiengesellschaft (AG) | Joint-stock Company |
| Societas Europaea (SE) | European Company |
| Europäische wirtschaftliche Interessenvereinigung (EWIV) | European Economic Interest Grouping |
| Societas Cooperativa Europaea (SCE) | European Cooperative Society |
| Genossenschaft | Cooperative Society |
| Gesellschaft bürgerlichen Rechts (eGbR) | Registered Civil Law Partnership |
| Eingetragener Kaufmann | Registered merchant |

### Accepted documents {#company-documents}

| Document type (German) | Description | Details |
|---|---|---|
| Nachweis der Firmenregistrierung* | Proof of Company Registration* | <ul><li>Must be issued by the Handelsregister/Transparenzregister.</li></ul> |
| Gesellschaftsvertrag / Satzungen | Articles of Association | <ul><li>Must be stamped and signed by a notary</li><li>Or uploaded from the official source.</li><li>Must be the most recently published version, or dated within the last **6 months** if amendments have been made.</li></ul> |
| UBO-Zertifikat* | Ultimate Beneficial Owners (UBO) certificate* | <ul><li>Must include all direct and indirect UBOs (ownership ≥25%).</li></ul> |
| Personalausweis der wirtschaftlich Berechtigten (UBOs) | Identity documents of UBOs | <ul><li>Valid identity documents in color</li><li>Both sides if applicable.</li></ul> |
| Vollmacht <br /> Personalausweis | Power of Attorney and ID of the legal representative | <ul><li>Required if the legal representative is not the account holder.</li></ul> |
| Formular für politisch exponierte Personen (PEP) | Politically Exposed Person (PEP) form | <ul><li>Will be sent to the end user (based on communication preferences).</li><li>Must be dated and signed.</li></ul> |
| Finanzberichte (z.B. Steuererklärung) | Financial statements | <ul><li>Most recent annual report or balance sheet.</li></ul> |
| Eidesstattliche Erklärung | Sworn statement | <ul><li>Must be signed and dated within the last 3 months.</li></ul> |
| Organigramm des Unternehmens | Company structure chart | <ul><li>An internal company document showing the holding structure.</li></ul> |

## Self-employed individuals and freelancers {#self-employed}

### Legal forms {#self-employed-types}

| Legal form (German) | Description |
|---|---|
| Freiberufler | Liberal professionals or freelancers |
| Selbständig/Gewerbetreibende | Self-employed in trade or business |
| Partnerschaft | Partnership |
| Gesellschaft bürgerlichen Rechts (GbR) | Civil law partnership |

### Accepted documents {#self-employed-documents}

| Document type (German) | Description | Details |
|---|---|---|
| Gewerbeschein* | Business or trade license* | <ul><li>Required for self-employed in trade or business</li><li>Also for GbR, if available</li><li>Must be issued by local authorities and dated.</li></ul> |
| Berufsanmeldung or Berufszulassung* | Professional registration or professional license* | <ul><li>Required for regulated liberal professionals or freelancers.</li></ul> |
| Freiberufler-registrierung* | Freelancer registration* | <ul><li>Required for liberal professionals or freelancers.</li></ul> |
| Satzung der Gesellschaft | Articles of Association | <ul><li>For Partnerships (Partnerschaft).</li><li>Must be dated and signed.</li><li>Must be the most recently published version.</li><li>If amendments have been made, it must be dated within the last **6 months**.</li></ul> |
| Bescheinigung über die steuerliche Erfassung* | Tax registration certificate* | <ul><li>Required for liberal professionals or freelancers</li><li>Issued by the Finanzamt.</li></ul> |
| Brief vom Finanzamt* | Letter from the tax office* | <ul><li>Letter confirming tax ID or VAT number.</li></ul> |
| Steuerbescheid | Tax declaration (most recent) | <ul><li>Must be issued by the tax office and dated within the last year.</li></ul> |

## Associations {#associations}

### Legal forms {#association-types}

| Legal form (German) | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Eingetragener Verein (e.V.) | Registered association |
| Nicht eingetragener Verein | Unregistered association |
| Gemeinnütziger Verein | Charitable association |
| Stiftung | Foundation |

### Accepted documents {#association-documents}

| Document type (German) | Description | Details |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Auszug aus dem Vereinsregister\* | Proof of registration\* | <ul><li>Must be issued by the Vereinsregister (association register).</li><li>Must be the latest available extract.</li><li>Confirm there are no dissolution or liquidation flags.</li><li>Retrieved by Swan when available; otherwise provided by the account holder.</li></ul> |
| Vereinssatzung\* | Articles of Association (statutes)\* | <ul><li>Must be dated and signed.</li><li>Must be the most recently published version.</li><li>If amendments have been made, it must be dated within the last **6 months**.</li><li>Provided by the account holder.</li></ul> |
| Gründungsprotokoll / Errichtungsurkunde | Founding deed | <ul><li>Required when founding documents aren't included in the statutes or register extract.</li><li>Provided by the account holder.</li></ul> |
| Protokoll der Mitgliederversammlung | General meeting minutes | <ul><li>Required when governance or representation powers aren't clear from the register extract.</li><li>Must be the latest version.</li><li>Should show names, roles, effective mandate date, and signatures.</li><li>Provided by the account holder.</li></ul> |
| Vorstandswahlprotokoll | Board or president appointment decision | <ul><li>Required when the register extract doesn't clearly show current board members or representation powers.</li><li>Provided by the account holder.</li></ul> |
| Organisationsstruktur / Organigramm | Governance structure documentation | <ul><li>Required when the association's governance structure is complex or not clear from other documents.</li><li>Provided by the account holder.</li></ul> |
| Mitgliederliste / Aufnahmeordnung | Membership list / admission rules | <ul><li>Provided by the account holder.</li></ul> |
| Personalausweis / Reisepass des Kontoverwalters\* | Account administrator identity document\* | <ul><li>Must be a valid identity document in color.</li><li>Both sides, if applicable.</li><li>Provided by the account holder.</li></ul> |
| Identitätsprüfung des Kontoverwalters\* | Account administrator identity verification\* | <ul><li>Provided via eID, PostIdent, VideoIdent, or expert verification.</li><li>Provided by the account holder.</li></ul> |
| Personalausweis des gesetzlichen Vertreters\* | Identity document of the legal representative\* | <ul><li>Must be a valid identity document in color.</li><li>Both sides, if applicable.</li><li>Provided by the account holder.</li></ul> |
| Vertretungsbescheinigung\* | Legal representative authority\* | <ul><li>Must confirm representation powers granted to the legal representative.</li><li>Must be dated.</li><li>Provided by the account holder.</li></ul> |
| Vollmacht zur Kontoeröffnung | Power of Attorney | <ul><li>Required when the person opening the account is not a legal board member (*Vorstand*).</li><li>The Power of Attorney must clearly grant account-opening and management powers.</li><li>Must be signed by the correct legal representative(s).</li><li>Must be dated within the last year.</li><li>Provided by the account holder.</li></ul> |
| Transparenzregisterauszug | UBO declaration (transparency register extract) | <ul><li>Provided by the account holder.</li></ul> |
| Mittelherkunftsnachweis | Source of funds evidence | <ul><li>Provided by the account holder.</li></ul> |
| Aktivitäts- und Finanzierungsfragebogen | Activity and funding questionnaire | <ul><li>Provided by the account holder.</li></ul> |
| Finanzberichte / Jahresabschluss | Annual accounts / financial statements | <ul><li>Most recent annual report or balance sheet.</li><li>Provided by the account holder.</li></ul> |
| Kontoauszüge | Bank statements | <ul><li>3-6 months of statements.</li><li>Provided by the account holder.</li></ul> |
| Spender- / Begünstigtenregister | Donor / beneficiary registers | <ul><li>Provided by the account holder.</li></ul> |
| Formular für politisch exponierte Personen (PEP) | Politically Exposed Person (PEP) form | <ul><li>Sent to the user (based on your communication preferences).</li><li>Must be dated and signed.</li></ul> |

## Homeowners associations {#homeowners-associations}

:::info Document retrieval for homeowners associations
German homeowners associations (WEG) aren't registered in the Handelsregister, so Swan can't retrieve their documents. The account holder provides all documents listed in this section.
:::

### Legal forms {#homeowners-types}

| Legal form (German) | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Wohnungseigentümergemeinschaft (WEG) | Homeowners association |

### Accepted documents {#homeowners-documents}

| Document type (German) | Description | Details |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Grundbuchauszug\* | Proof of registration\* | <ul><li>Official registration document showing the homeowners association is active.</li><li>Must be dated.</li><li>Provided by the account holder.</li></ul> |
| Teilungserklärung | Declaration of division | <ul><li>The founding document of the homeowners association.</li><li>Must be the most recently published version.</li><li>Provided by the account holder.</li></ul> |
| Protokoll der Eigentümerversammlung\* | Owners' meeting minutes\* | <ul><li>Must be the most recent, within the last **2 years**.</li><li>Should show the appointed property manager (Verwalter) or board members, their roles, and signatures.</li><li>Provided by the account holder.</li></ul> |
| Verwaltervertrag oder Bestellungsbeschluss | Property manager contract or appointment resolution | <ul><li>Required to confirm who manages the association and has authority to open an account.</li><li>Must be signed and dated.</li><li>Provided by the account holder.</li></ul> |
| Vollmacht und Personalausweis des Kontoeröffners | Power of Attorney and ID of the account opener | <ul><li>Required when the account opener isn't the property manager or a directly authorized board member.</li><li>The Power of Attorney must clearly grant account-opening and management powers.</li><li>Must be signed by the correct representative(s).</li><li>Must be dated within the last year.</li><li>Provided by the account holder.</li></ul> |
| Personalausweis des gesetzlichen Vertreters\* | Identity document of the legal representative\* | <ul><li>Must be a valid identity document in color.</li><li>Both sides, if applicable.</li><li>Provided by the account holder.</li></ul> |

:::warning Additional documentation
Depending on the company type, business activity, and applicable regulations, Swan may request additional documentation at any stage of the account lifecycle. The documents listed on these pages are standard requirements — not an exhaustive list.
:::
