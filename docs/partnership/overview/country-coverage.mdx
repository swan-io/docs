# Country coverage

Swan's e-money license is valid **across the European Economic Area** (EEA).
The availability of Swan's features depends on local regulations and the presence of the provider in each country.

## French overseas territories {#french-overseas-territories}

France's overseas territories have varying eligibility for Swan accounts based on their legal status and relationship to the European Union.

### Eligible DROM territories {#eligible-drom}

The following French overseas departments and regions (DROM) are **part of the EU/EEA** and **are eligible** for Swan accounts:

- Guadeloupe
- Martinique
- French Guiana
- La RÃ©union
- Mayotte

### Ineligible COM territories {#ineligible-com}

The following French overseas collectivities (COM) are **not part of the EU/EEA** and are **not eligible** for Swan accounts:

- French Polynesia
- Wallis-et-Futuna
- Saint-Pierre-et-Miquelon
- Saint-BarthÃ©lemy

### Special status territories {#special-status}

The following French overseas territories are **not eligible** for Swan accounts:

- New Caledonia (sui generis status, not part of EU/EEA)
- French Southern and Antarctic Lands (TAAF) (no permanent population)
- Scattered Islands (no permanent population)

### Address requirements for DROM {#drom-addresses}

When creating accounts for entities or individuals in eligible DROM territories:

1. **Country selection**: Select **France** (`FRA`) as the country for all DROM addresses.
1. **Applies to**: Both company addresses and Ultimate Beneficial Owner (UBO) addresses.
1. **Proper formatting**: Use standard French address formatting with the territory name in the address lines.

### Physical card shipping to DROM {#drom-card-shipping}

Swan ships physical cards to all eligible DROM territories:

- **Shipping route**: Cards are shipped out of [Swan's France hub](/topics/cards/physical/#hubs-france).
- **Carrier**: La Poste handles delivery to DROM territories.
- **Delivery times**: Standard DROM delivery times apply (typically longer than metropolitan France).
