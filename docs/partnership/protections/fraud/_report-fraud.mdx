If your users are victims of fraud with their Swan payment accounts, they **must** file a [fraud dispute with Swan](https://supportform.swan.io/).
You can also [alert Swan](https://partner-support.swan.io/hc/en-gb/requests/new) to suspected fraudulent activity.

:::danger Critical
Reporting fraud to Swan is **urgent** and **mandatory**.
:::

Swan processes fraud disputes based on criticality.
All fraud disputes are analyzed, but they're not all accepted.
Swan responds as quickly as possible.
