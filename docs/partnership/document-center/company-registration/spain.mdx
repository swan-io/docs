### Accepted documents {#companies-documents}
The following table lists the proof of registration documents accepted for companies registered in Spain.
:::info Document request
*Swan retrieves documents marked with an asterisk independently. 
All other documents may be requested from your end users on a case-by-case basis. 
:::

| Document type (Spanish) | Description | Details |
|---|---|---|
| Certificado de inscripción* | Proof of company registration* | <ul><li>Retrieved by Swan</li><li>Official document</li><li>Dated</li></ul> |
| Certificación de estatutos sociales* | Articles of association* | <ul><li>Dated and signed</li></ul> |
| Certificado de beneficiario efectivo | Ultimate Beneficial Owner (UBO) certificate | <ul><li>Official document</li><li>Signed and stamped</li></ul> |
| Documento de identidad de los UBOs | Identity document of UBOs | <ul><li>Official and valid document</li></ul> |
| Justificante de domicilio de los UBOs | Proof of address of UBOs | <ul><li>Proof of address must be dated within the last 3 months</li></ul> |
| Poder Notarial: Apoderado General or Apoderado Especial, and <br/> documento de identidad of the legal representative | Power of Attorney: general or special attorney, and <br/> identity document of the legal representative | <ul><li>Must be dated (within the last year) and signed</li><li>Identity document of the legal representative (if they are not the account holder)</li></ul> |
| Formulario de Persona Expuesta Políticamente (PEP) | Politically Exposed Person (PEP) form | <ul><li>Will be sent to the end user (based on your communication preferences)</li><li>Must be signed and dated</li></ul> |
| Estados financieros | Financial statements | <ul><li>Dated within the last 3 months</li></ul> |
| Declaración jurada | Sworn statement | <ul><li>Signed</li><li>Dated within the last 3 months</li></ul> |
| Organigrama de la empresa | Company structure chart | <ul><li>Internal company document</li></ul> |

## Self-employed individuals and freelancers {#self-employed}

### Legal forms {#self-employed-types}
The following table describes the legal forms relevant to self-employed individuals and freelancers in Spain.

| Legal form (Spanish)        | Description             |
|---|---|
| Empresario Individual (Autónomo) | Self-employed professionals |
| Profesionales liberales     | Freelance professionals   |

### Accepted documents {#self-employed-documents}
The following table lists the accepted proof of registration documents for self-employed individuals and freelancers in Spain.

:::info Document request
*Documents marked with an asterisk are mandatory.
All other documents may be requested on a case-by-case basis.
:::

| Document type (Spanish) | Description | Details |
|---|---|---|
| Certificado de Alta de Autónomo* | Proof of registration* | <ul><li>Official document</li><li>Dated</li></ul> |
| Modelo 036 or Modelo 037 | Tax registration form | <ul><li>Official document</li><li>Dated</li></ul> |
| Documento de identidad | Identity document | <ul><li>Required for freelancers</li></ul> |
| Primera transferencia bancaria | First transfer | <ul><li>Must be from a bank account in the name of the client</li><li>Bank account must be within the European Economic Area (EEA)</li><li>Not required for Spanish IBANs (unless otherwise stated)</li></ul> |
| Justificante de domicilio | Proof of address | <ul><li>Dated within the last 3 months</li></ul> |
| Declaración de la renta | Income tax return | <ul><li>Most recent (for the previous year)</li></ul> |
| Poder Notarial and the documento de identidad of the legal representative | Power of attorney and identity document of the legal representative | <ul><li>Must be dated (within the last year) and signed</li><li>Identity document of the legal representative (if they are not the account holder)</li></ul> |
| Formulario de Persona Expuesta Políticamente (PEP) | Politically Exposed Person (PEP) form | <ul><li>Will be sent to the end user (based on your communication preferences)</li><li>Must be signed and dated</li></ul> |
| Justificante de ingresos | Proof of income | <ul><li>Tax return or pay slip are accepted</li></ul> |

## Associations and homeowners associations {#associations}

### Legal forms {#associations-types}
The following table describes the legal forms typically found for associations and homeowners associations registered in Spain.

| Legal form (Spanish)     | Description        |
|---|---|
| Asociaciones             | Associations               |
| Comunidad de Propietarios | Homeowners association (HOA)    |

### Accepted documents {#associations-documents}
The following table lists the accepted proof of registration documents for associations and homeowners associations in Spain.

:::info Document request
*Documents marked with an asterisk are mandatory.
All other documents may be requested on a case-by-case basis. 
:::

| Document type (Spanish) | Description | Details |
|---|---|---|
| Certificación de inscripción en el Registro de Asociaciones Autonómico o en el Registro Nacional de Asociaciones* | Certification of registration in the Autonomous Registry of Associations or in the National Registry of Associations* | <ul><li>Applies to associations</li></ul> |
| Acta Fundacional* | Founding act* | <ul><li>Applies to associations</li></ul> |
| Documento de identidad | Identity document* | <ul><li>Applies to associations</li><li>Required for all members and the person opening the account</li></ul> |
| Acta de la Última junta or Certificado de Cargos* | Meeting minutes or a certificate of positions or roles* | <ul><li>Applies to homeowners associations</li><li>Either one of these documents will be accepted</li><li>Meeting minutes must be the latest ones with a maximum of 2 years</li></ul> |
| Tarjeta acreditativa de Número del Identificación Fiscal (NIF)* | Tax identification card* | <ul><li>Applies to homeowners associations</li><li>Dated within the last 3 months</li><li>Signed</li></ul> |
| Justificante de domicilio* | Proof of company address* | <ul><li>Dated within the last 3 months</li></ul> |
| Declaración de la Renta | Income tax return | <ul><li>Most recent (for the previous year)</li></ul> |
| Poder notarial and documento de identidad | Power of attorney and identity document | <ul><li>Dated within the last year</li><li>Identity document of the legal representative (if they are not the account holder)</li></ul> |
| Documento de identidad de los UBOs | Identity document of UBOs | <ul><li>Official and valid document</li></ul> |
| Justificante de domicilio de los UBOs | Proof of address of UBOs | <ul><li>Proof of address must be dated within the last 3 months</li></ul> |
| Formulario de Persona Expuesta Políticamente (PEP) | Politically Exposed Person (PEP) form | <ul><li>Will be sent to the end user</li><li>Must be signed and dated</li></ul> |
