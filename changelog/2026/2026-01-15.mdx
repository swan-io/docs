### Reminders

ğŸ“† On **April 1**, the following fields will be removed from the [`accountStatement`](https://api-reference.swan.io/queries/account-statement/) and [`Account.statements`](https://api-reference.swan.io/objects/account/) queries and from the [`generateAccountStatement`](https://api-reference.swan.io/mutations/generate-account-statement/) mutation response:

- `status` will be replaced by the `statusInfo` object.
- The statement download `url`, previously in the `type` object, will only be available in `statusInfo`.
