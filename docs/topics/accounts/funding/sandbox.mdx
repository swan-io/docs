# Sandbox: Account funding

## Simulate funding an account with a credit transfer {#simulate-fund-credit-transfer}

In Sandbox, simulate funding an account using the Event Simulator and a fake debtor.

1. Go to **Dashboard** > **Developers** > **Event Simulator** > **SEPA Credit Transfers**.
1. Go to the **Receive an incoming transfer** tab.
1. Enter the desired amount and your Swan IBAN, and which is instantly credited to your Sandbox account.
